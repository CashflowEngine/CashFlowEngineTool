# Framer AI Prompt - CashFlow Engine Landing Page

## Prompt (Copy & Paste für Framer AI)

---

**Create a professional SaaS landing page for "CashFlow Engine" - an advanced portfolio analytics and risk simulation platform for option traders.**

### Brand & Visual Identity

**Colors:**
- Primary: #302BFF (Electric Blue) - for CTAs, headers, accents
- Secondary: #00D2BE (Teal) - for positive indicators, success states
- Accent: #FF2E4D (Coral) - for highlights, urgency
- Background: #FFFFFF with #F9FAFB sections
- Text: #4B5563 (headlines), #6B7280 (body)
- Subtle accents: #F0F4FF (light blue tint backgrounds)

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Bold, uppercase, wide letter-spacing (like Exo 2 or similar tech font)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (like Poppins or Inter)

**Style:**
- Professional, data-driven aesthetic
- Clean with generous whitespace
- Subtle gradients and shadows
- Cards with light borders (1px #E5E7EB)
- Border radius: 8-12px

---

### Page Sections

**1. Hero Section**
- Headline: "Advanced Portfolio Analytics & Risk Simulation for Option Traders"
- Subheadline: "Professional-grade tools to analyze your options trading performance, stress-test your strategies, and optimize your portfolio."
- Primary CTA: "Get Started" (Electric Blue button)
- Secondary CTA: "See Features" (outline button)
- Background: Subtle gradient or abstract data visualization pattern

**2. Features Grid (8 Modules)**

Create a 2-row × 4-column grid of feature cards:

1. **Portfolio Analytics** (Icon: Chart/Graph)
   "Comprehensive deep dive into portfolio performance with KPIs, monthly return matrices, and equity growth curves."

2. **Portfolio Builder** (Icon: Building Blocks)
   "Construct and balance multi-strategy portfolios. Optimize capital allocation using Kelly Criterion and MART ratios."

3. **Monte Carlo Simulation** (Icon: Dice/Random)
   "Stress test against thousands of market scenarios. Simulate Black Swan events and analyze drawdown probabilities."

4. **Reality Check** (Icon: Compare/Diff)
   "Compare live trading vs. backtest results. Identify slippage and performance gaps."

5. **MEIC Deep Dive** (Icon: Target/Crosshair)
   "Specialized analysis for Multiple Entry Iron Condors. Optimize entry times and market conditions."

6. **MEIC Optimizer** (Icon: Sliders/Settings) - with "Beta" badge
   "Generate entry signals for Option Omega. Find the most robust parameter sets for your strategy."

7. **AI Analyst** (Icon: Brain/AI) - with "Coming Soon" badge
   "Ask questions about your portfolio using AI. Get data-driven insights and improvement suggestions."

8. **Documentation** (Icon: Book/Guide) - with "Coming Soon" badge
   "Comprehensive guides on metrics interpretation and mathematical models."

**3. How It Works Section**
Three-step process with numbered circles:

1. **Import Data** - "Upload your Option Omega backtest exports or live trading logs (CSV/XLSX)"
2. **Analyze** - "Choose from 8 professional analysis modules tailored for option traders"
3. **Optimize** - "Save analyses to cloud, compare strategies, and refine your approach"

**4. Key Metrics Section**
Show example metric cards in a row:
- "Total P&L" with sample value
- "Win Rate" with percentage
- "Sharpe Ratio" with value
- "Max Drawdown" with percentage

Use color coding: Teal (#00D2BE) for positive metrics, Coral (#FF2E4D) for risk metrics.

**5. Social Proof / Trust Section**
- "Built by traders, for traders"
- "Used by serious option traders worldwide"
- Highlight: Monte Carlo simulations, Kelly Criterion optimization, professional-grade analytics

**6. CTA Section**
- Headline: "Ready to Level Up Your Trading Analytics?"
- Subheadline: "Import your first backtest and see the difference professional tools make."
- Large CTA button: "Start Analyzing Now"

**7. Footer**
- Logo
- Legal disclaimer: "This application is for educational and entertainment purposes only. It does not constitute financial advice. Option trading involves significant risk."
- Copyright

---

### Additional Notes

- Make it feel like a premium fintech product
- Use data visualization elements as decorative backgrounds (charts, grids, numbers)
- Include subtle hover animations on cards and buttons
- Mobile responsive design
- Dark mode version would use #0F1117 background with same accent colors
